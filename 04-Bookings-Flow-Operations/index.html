    Module 04: Bookings Flow & Operations¶

    Module Duration: 75 minutes | Difficulty: Intermediate Prerequisites: Module 03 completed, admin or guide role required

    üéØ PURPOSE¶

    This module teaches you the complete booking lifecycle from inquiry to completion. You'll learn: - How bookings move through statuses (pending ‚Üí confirmed ‚Üí completed/cancelled) - Real-time availability checking and manual overrides - Refund and partial refund procedures - Edge cases and troubleshooting - Monthly reconciliation checklist

    ‚ö†Ô∏è Critical: Bookings directly affect revenue and customer experience. Follow procedures exactly.

    ‚è∞ WHEN TO USE¶

    Use this module when: - Processing daily booking confirmations - Handling customer booking modifications - Issuing refunds or cancellations - Investigating booking discrepancies - Performing month-end booking reconciliation - Training new operations staff

    üìä BOOKING LIFECYCLE DIAGRAM¶

    Customer Books Tour ‚Üí PENDING (payment processing)
           ‚Üì
    Payment Success ‚Üí CONFIRMED (send WhatsApp confirmation)
           ‚Üì
  Tour Date Arrives ‚Üí IN_PROGRESS (day of tour)
           ‚Üì
Tour Completes ‚Üí COMPLETED (trigger payout to guide, request review)

Alternative Paths:
PENDING ‚Üí Payment Failed ‚Üí CANCELLED (auto, no refund)
CONFIRMED ‚Üí Customer Cancels ‚Üí CANCELLED (refund based on policy)
CONFIRMED ‚Üí Guide Cancels ‚Üí CANCELLED (full refund + credit)

    Status Definitions:

    Status Meaning Actions Available
    pending Payment processing (< 5 min usually) Monitor, do not modify
    confirmed Paid, awaiting tour date Modify, cancel (refund policy)
    in_progress Tour happening today Check-in travelers, mark complete
    completed Tour finished, payment settled Request review, handle complaints
    cancelled Booking voided (various reasons) View only (cannot resurrect)
    refunded Money returned to customer View refund details

    üõ†Ô∏è STEP-BY-STEP: Process Daily Bookings¶

    Morning Routine (Every Day at 8:00 AM)¶

    Step 1: Review Pending Bookings¶

    1. Admin Dashboard ‚Üí "Bookings" ‚Üí Filter: "Pending"
    2. Check for bookings older than 10 minutes:
    3. Stripe payment may have failed
    4. Customer abandoned checkout
    5. Payment authorization issue

    Action for Stuck Pending: - If < 30 minutes old: Wait (payment processors can be slow) - If > 30 minutes old: Contact customer via email (template: "Payment Incomplete") - If > 24 hours old: Auto-cancel (system does this automatically)

    Step 2: Confirm Today's Tours¶

    1. Dashboard ‚Üí "Bookings" ‚Üí Filter: "In Progress" + "Tour Date = Today"
    2. For each booking:
    3. [ ] Verify guide has been notified (auto WhatsApp sent at 6 AM)
    4. [ ] Check weather conditions (if outdoor activity)
    5. [ ] Confirm meeting point logistics
    6. [ ] Note any special requests (dietary, mobility)

    Red Flags: - No guide confirmation by 2 hours before tour ‚Üí Call guide directly - Severe weather warning ‚Üí Contact guide about cancellation - Customer no-show ‚Üí Wait 30 min past meeting time, then mark as "customer_no_show"

    Step 3: Send Confirmation Reminders¶

    For tours happening tomorrow:

    1. Dashboard ‚Üí "Bookings" ‚Üí Filter: "Tour Date = Tomorrow" + "Confirmed"
    2. System auto-sends WhatsApp reminder at 4 PM (verify in Logs)
    3. Manually check:
    4. [ ] WhatsApp delivery status = "delivered" (green checkmark)
    5. [ ] If failed: Retry via email (backup method)

    üí° Tip: 80% of no-shows are prevented by 24-hour reminders.

    üí∞ Refund Procedures¶

    Refund Policy Matrix¶

    Cancellation Timing Refund Amount Who Authorizes
    > 14 days before tour 100% refund Auto-approved
    7-14 days before tour 50% refund Admin approval
    2-6 days before tour 25% refund Admin approval
    < 2 days before tour No refund Super admin exception
    Guide cancels (any time) 100% + $50 credit Auto-approved
    Weather cancellation 100% or reschedule Auto-approved
    Customer no-show No refund N/A

    ‚ö†Ô∏è Caution: Deviations from this policy require written CTO approval.

    Step-by-Step: Issue Refund¶

    Prerequisites¶

    • Admin or super_admin role
    • Access to Stripe Dashboard (credentials from finance team)
    • Booking ID and original payment amount verified

    Process¶

    1. Verify Eligibility
    2. Check refund policy matrix above
    3. Confirm no previous refund issued (prevent double refunds)

    4. Screenshot booking details for audit trail

    5. Calculate Refund Amount

    6. Full refund: Original payment amount
    7. Partial refund: Calculate based on policy (e.g., 50% = $250 ‚Üí $125)

    8. Processing fees: Stripe fees ($3 + 2.9%) are NON-REFUNDABLE (explain to customer)

    9. Process in Stripe Dashboard

    10. Log into Stripe Dashboard ‚Üí "Payments"
    11. Search by booking reference (e.g., "BOOK-2025-12345")
    12. Click payment ‚Üí "Refund" button
    13. Enter refund amount (USD, no commas)
    14. Reason dropdown: Select appropriate category

    15. Click "Refund $XXX.XX"

    16. Update Booking Status

    17. Return to Admin Dashboard
    18. Find booking ‚Üí Click "‚ãÆ More" ‚Üí "Mark as Refunded"
    19. Enter refund details:
      • Refund amount
      • Refund reason (customer dropdown)
      • Internal notes (why exception granted, if applicable)

    20. Click "Save Refund"

    21. Notify Customer

    22. System auto-sends email: "Your refund of $XXX.XX has been processed"
    23. Funds appear in customer's account within 5-10 business days
    24. Add note: "If refund not received after 10 days, contact bank"

    ‚úÖ Success: Booking status shows "refunded", audit log entry created, customer notification sent.

    Partial Refund Example¶

    Scenario: Customer cancels 10 days before $300 tour.

    Calculation: - Original payment: $300.00 - Refund policy: 50% (7-14 days window) - Refund amount: $150.00 - Customer keeps: $150.00 (covers guide's time, admin costs)

    Important: Explain to customer via email: 

    Hi [Customer Name],

Your cancellation falls within our 7-14 day window, which qualifies for a 50% refund per our policy.

Refund amount: $150.00 (will appear in 5-10 business days)
Non-refundable amount: $150.00 (covers guide preparation and admin costs)

Thank you for understanding. We'd love to welcome you on a future adventure!

Best,
LetsGoBelize.app Team

    üîÑ Manual Overrides (Use with Caution)¶

    When Manual Intervention Required¶

    1. Availability Override
    2. Scenario: Customer wants to book tour that shows "sold out"
    3. Reason: Guide confirmed they can take 1 more person
    4. Action: Admin increases max_participants by 1 for that date

    5. Risk: Tour quality may suffer if too crowded

    6. Price Override

    7. Scenario: VIP customer negotiated discounted rate
    8. Reason: Corporate partnership, repeat customer loyalty
    9. Action: Admin creates booking with custom price

    10. Risk: Revenue loss, other customers may request same discount

    11. Date Change Without Penalty

    12. Scenario: Customer needs to reschedule due to emergency
    13. Reason: Compassionate exception (e.g., family medical emergency)
    14. Action: Admin transfers booking to new date, waives change fee
    15. Risk: Sets precedent, others may request same

    ‚ö†Ô∏è Approval Required: All manual overrides require super admin written approval via email.

    Override Procedure¶

    1. Customer requests exception ‚Üí Document in writing (email/ticket)
    2. Admin evaluates request ‚Üí Check refund policy, guide availability
    3. Request super admin approval ‚Üí Forward customer email + recommendation
    4. If approved:
    5. Admin processes override in dashboard
    6. Add detailed note to booking: "Exception granted by [Super Admin Name] on [Date] - Reason: [Justification]"
    7. Log in monthly audit report
    8. If denied:
    9. Politely explain policy to customer
    10. Offer alternative (e.g., credit toward future booking)

    üí° Audit Trail: All overrides are logged and reviewed quarterly.

    ‚ö†Ô∏è DO / DO NOT¶

    DO:¶

    ‚úÖ Verify payment success before confirming tours - Check Stripe Dashboard for "succeeded" status - Never manually confirm bookings with "pending" payments

    ‚úÖ Communicate proactively - Send 24-hour reminders - Notify customers of weather changes - Update guides on special requests

    ‚úÖ Document everything - Screenshot refund confirmations - Save customer emails requesting changes - Log all manual overrides

    ‚úÖ Follow refund policy strictly - Exceptions require super admin approval - Explain policy clearly to customers - Be empathetic but firm

    DO NOT:¶

    ‚ùå Never issue refunds > $500 without super admin approval - Security policy (August 23, 2025 audit) - High-value refunds trigger fraud alerts

    ‚ùå Never change booking details without customer confirmation - Always email customer before modifying dates, times, or tours - Get written "yes" before processing

    ‚ùå Never delete bookings - Use "cancel" status instead (preserves audit trail) - Deletions trigger security investigation

    ‚ùå Never bypass Stripe for direct bank transfers - All refunds MUST go through Stripe - Direct transfers violate payment processor terms

    ‚úÖ VERIFICATION CHECKLIST¶

    After processing a booking action:

    • [ ] Booking status updated correctly in database
    • [ ] Customer notified via email/WhatsApp
    • [ ] Guide notified (if tour-related change)
    • [ ] Stripe payment status matches booking status
    • [ ] Audit log entry created
    • [ ] Manual override (if any) documented with approval
    • [ ] Refund (if any) confirmed in Stripe Dashboard
    • [ ] No duplicate bookings created
    • [ ] Calendar availability updated (if date changed)

    üìã Monthly Reconciliation Checklist¶

    Perform on the 1st of each month:

    • [ ] Export all bookings from previous month (CSV)
    • [ ] Export all Stripe payments from previous month
    • [ ] Cross-reference: Every booking has matching payment
    • [ ] Identify discrepancies:
    • [ ] Bookings without payments (investigate immediately)
    • [ ] Payments without bookings (possible fraud)
    • [ ] Refunds without "refunded" status
    • [ ] Calculate total revenue: Sum of all "completed" bookings
    • [ ] Calculate total refunds: Sum of all "refunded" bookings
    • [ ] Net revenue: Revenue - Refunds
    • [ ] Compare to Stripe Dashboard balance (should match within $10)
    • [ ] Document any discrepancies > $50
    • [ ] Email finance team: "Monthly Booking Reconciliation - [Month]"
